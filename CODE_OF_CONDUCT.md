# Code of Conduct

We want Feishu Bot to be a practical, welcoming project for people building local-first Feishu automation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive.
- Focus feedback on code, design, documentation, and user impact.
- Assume good intent, especially across languages and cultures.
- Keep examples, logs, and screenshots free of private company data and credentials.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private credentials, internal data, user identifiers, or logs without consent.
- Repeated off-topic, spammy, or disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may lead to blocked participation in the repository.

For sensitive conduct or security concerns, contact the repository owner privately instead of opening a public issue.
